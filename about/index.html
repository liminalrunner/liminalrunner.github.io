about

Welcome to my about page. I am 34 years old (as of 2020). In spring of 2018 I did c25k to help with losing weight, because my diet was stalling. I completed that and continued to run for a few months but an injury stopped me from running for a while and after that I did not pick it up again, because I had zero fun doing it. A major reason for that was that I had no real concept of an easy pace and was doing these 5k runs heaving for air all the way. I basically didn’t pick running up again.
That is until this year. I basically started again from zero (not that I was anywhere “fast” before) and this time decided to use a 5k Training Plan i found on the Nike website, which is based on the Audio Guided Runs in their Nike Run Club app, which I think is a neat thing and works for me.
Running gets me to move, which I don’t do a lot of the rest of the day. At the moment I am still following this 5k Training Plan and if the weather doesn’t interfere with storms and heavy rain, I plan to run 4 to 5 times per week.

I know I am slow, but everyone has to start at some point, very slow is just that starting point for me. I don’t have any particular time/pace/distance goal in mind, but at some point running a marathon (if I can commit enough time to actually train for one) in the far future would be nice. But so far I am having fun and am taking it easy, going step by step, hence the name of this page.

This page will serve as a journal for my running activities and maybe also keep me accountable to not just stop it again.
