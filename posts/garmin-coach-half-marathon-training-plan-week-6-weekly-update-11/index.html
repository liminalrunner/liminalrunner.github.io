Garmin Coach Half-Marathon Week 6/16 | Weekly Update #11

Aug 22, 2020 in GARMIN COACH HALF-MARATHON TRAINING PLAN • HALF-MARATHON • WEEKLY UPDATE
half-marathon training plan garmin coach garmin connect coach amy
3 min read

New week with 4 new runs, who expected that?

This weeks runs:

  • Monday (17th of August): Tired Run
  • Tuesday (18th of August): Easy Run
  • Thursday (20th of August): Easy Run
  • Saturday (22nd of August): Run

Same pattern of runs as last week. Hoping for some mix up soon.

Monday Run

  • Warm up: 2 min | Walk or jog lightly
  • Run: 6.44km | Give your best effort on tired legs. Don’t overdo it.
  • Cool down: 2 min | Walk or jog lightly

I kept this run on monday, since this seem to be what the plan wants. I honestly did not feel tired at all and tried to stay near the same pace as the other easy runs for a total distance of 6.82km.

Tuesday Run

  • Warm up: 2 min | Walk or jog lightly
  • Run: 3.22km | 8:31 to 8:50 min/km | Easy pace.
  • Cool down: 2 min | Walk or jog lightly

I assume since the Monday run was longer, it turned down the Tuesday run a bit. An easy run over 3.55km.

Thursday Run

  • Warm up: 2 min | Walk or jog lightly
  • Run: 4.83km | 8:31 to 8:50 min/km | Easy pace.
  • Cool down: 2 min | Walk or jog lightly

Not much to say about it. Distance: 5.20km

Saturday Run

  • Warm up: 2 min | Walk or jog lightly
  • Run: 9.65km | 8:31 to 8:50 min/km | Easy pace.
  • Cool down: 2 min | Walk or jog lightly

The long run went back to the 9.65 distance this week. Maybe because I rated last weeks long run as “Hard”, but who knows. No struggles here, for 10.05km.

This brings this weeks total distance to 25.62km/15.92miles, around the same as 2 weeks ago. So a step back in distance. I am wondering if this is just normal for the plan or a reaction to last weeks long run.

This is 6 weeks done and 10 to go in this plan.
