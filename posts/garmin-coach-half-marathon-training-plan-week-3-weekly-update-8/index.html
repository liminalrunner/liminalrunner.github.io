Garmin Coach Half-Marathon Week 3/16 | Weekly Update #8

Aug 02, 2020 in GARMIN COACH HALF-MARATHON TRAINING PLAN • HALF-MARATHON • WEEKLY UPDATE
half-marathon training plan shoes garmin coach garmin connect coach amy hoka one one gaviota 2
3 min read

Week 3 of 16 in my Garmin Coach Half-Marathon Training plan is completed! This week I also wore the Hoka One One Gaviota 2 for all runs.

Training Plan

This weeks runs:

  • Monday (27th of July): Run
  • Wednesday (29th of July): Run
  • Thursday (30th of July): Run
  • Saturday (1st of August): Run

Monday Run

  • Warm up: 2 min | Walk or jog lightly
  • Run: 4.83km | 8:31 to 8:50 min/km | Easy pace.
  • Cool down: 2 min | Walk or jog lightly

Somepoint last week, Garmins Server for all the online/sync stuff went down and still were on Monday. I still had the workout on the watch, but after it syncing worked again and it got uploaded, it didn’t register as the run that I should’ve done for the plan, so the plan assumes I missed 1 run this week. Doesn’t really matter to me, because I know I did the workout.
It just felt really weird how Garmins Connect App/Web Service works. Basically nothing is done on your Smartphone, you could not sync from smartphone to watch or reverse without a connection to the Garmin Servers.

Overall distance: 5.14km

Wednesday Run

  • Warm up: 2 min | Walk or jog lightly
  • Run: 3.22km | 8:31 to 8:50 min/km | Easy pace.
  • Cool down: 2 min | Walk or jog lightly

Nice short run. Distance: 4.35km

Thursday Run

  • Warm up: 2 min | Walk or jog lightly
  • Run: 4.83km | 8:31 to 8:50 min/km | Easy pace.
  • Cool down: 2 min | Walk or jog lightly

Same as Monday length as Monday with a distance of 5.11km this time

Saturday Run

  • Warm up: 2 min | Walk or jog lightly
  • Run: 9.65km | 8:31 to 8:50 min/km | Easy pace.
  • Cool down: 2 min | Walk or jog lightly

This weeks Long Run was the same distance as last weeks, on this day I ran a total of 10.27km.

My weekly distance was therefore around 24.87km / 15,45 miles, which is a little bit less than a mile more than last week.

Shoe

As I said above, I wore the new Gaviota 2 for every run this week. They feel just right. Nothing more or less to add, very happy with that purchase!
