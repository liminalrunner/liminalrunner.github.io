Garmin Coach Half-Marathon Week 2/16 | Weekly Update #7

Jul 26, 2020 in GARMIN COACH HALF-MARATHON TRAINING PLAN • HALF-MARATHON • WEEKLY UPDATE
half-marathon training plan shoes garmin coach garmin connect coach amy hoka one one gaviota 2
4 min read

Second week of 16 of my Garmin Coach Half-Marathon Training plan done! All the runs and stats below. Also received a pair of the Hoka One One Gaviota 2 that I tried out last week.

Training Plan

This was the first week with 4 workouts:

  • Monday (20th of July): Run
  • Tuesday (21th of July): Run
  • Thursday (23th of July): Run
  • Saturday (25th of July): Run

Monday Run

  • Warm up: 2 min | Walk or jog lightly
  • Run: 4.02km | 8:31 to 8:50 min/km | Easy pace.
  • Cool down: 2 min | Walk or jog lightly

This was an easy effort over 4.46km. I still have a hard time keeping the same pace. The Garmin Forerunner 245 shows you the “pace window” that you should stay in but it kept beeping and vibrating because at the beginning of the run I was running faster than 8:31 min/km.

Tuesday Run

  • Warm up: 2 min | Walk or jog lightly
  • Run: 3.22km | 8:31 to 8:50 min/km | Easy pace.
  • Cool down: 2 min | Walk or jog lightly

Another easy one over the distance of 3.68km.

Thursday Run

  • Warm up: 2 min | Walk or jog lightly
  • Run: 4.83km | 8:31 to 8:50 min/km | Easy pace.
  • Cool down: 2 min | Walk or jog lightly

This is the same distance as last weeks Thursday run. Total distance for this one was 5.20km.

Saturday Run

  • Warm up: 2 min | Walk or jog lightly
  • Run: 9.65km | 8:31 to 8:50 min/km | Easy pace.
  • Cool down: 2 min | Walk or jog lightly

Last weeks Long Run was over 8.05km / 5 Miles, this week was a step up of a Mile to 9.65km / 6 Miles which is my newest farthest distance. The complete distance for this run was exactly 10.00km. I rated this as a “hard” workout, because at the end of this run is was done. With this I also set a new Personal Record for the 10k distance with it being my only run to reach 10k so far with 1h28min09s.

For this whole week I had a distance of 23.34km / 14.5 Miles. So I ran the half-marathon, just stretched out over the week. I am pretty happy with this week. Looking forward to running more!

Hoka One One Gaviota 2

My pair of the Gaviota 2 (EU Size 44, Wide) arrived and I did the Thursday run in them. There is a huge difference to my Asics Gel-Fortitude 8, it just feels so much softer, in a good way. The upper material fits nicely without constricting my fat mid-foot. The soft cushioning will definitely help protecting my knees from the impact, especially on the longer runs in the coming weeks.

Don’t really have much more to say. See you next week!
