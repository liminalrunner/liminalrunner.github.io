Garmin Coach Half-Marathon Week 10/16 | Weekly Update #15

Sep 20, 2020 in GARMIN COACH HALF-MARATHON TRAINING PLAN • HALF-MARATHON • WEEKLY UPDATE
half-marathon training plan garmin coach garmin connect coach amy
3 min read

This week had only 3 runs, read on why.

This weeks runs:

  • Monday (14th of September): Tired Run
  • Tuesday (15th of September): Speed Repeats
  • Saturday (19th of September): Long Run

There was an Easy Run originally scheduled for Thursday, but I was sick from Wednesday till Friday and on Friday forgot to reschedule it to Sunday. Once the day that a run is scheduled on has passed, you cannot reschedule it on these Garmin Training Plans. I tried to reset it by changing the settings of the plan, but that did not work. Instead it made the Long Run from initially bein a 16.09km run into a 12.87km run.

Monday Run

  • Warm up: 2 min | Walk or jog lightly
  • Run: 8.05km | Give your best effort on tired legs. Don’t overdo it.
  • Cool down: 2 min | Walk or jog lightly

Distance: 8.35km

Tuesday Run

  • Warm up: 1.61km | Walk or jog lightly
  • 7 repeats:
    • Run: 2 min | 6:58 to 7:16 min/km | Run hard!
    • Recover: 2 min | Jog or walk if you need to.
  • Cool down: 1.61km | Walk or jog lightly

I like the longer intervals, makes it much easier to find the right pace. Distance: 6.60km

Saturday Run

  • Warm up: 2 min | Walk or jog lightly
  • Run: 12.87km | 8:31 to 8:50 min/km | Easy pace.
  • Cool down: 2 min | Walk or jog lightly

As I wrote above, by trying to mess with the training plan settings, it changed the long run to 12.87km instead of 16.09km. I just tried to run the distance it was supposed to be. Distance: 16.12km

In total I covered 31.16km this week, which would have been near 38 with the missed 6.44km run.
