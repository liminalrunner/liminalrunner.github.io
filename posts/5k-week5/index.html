Weekly Update #1

It is week 24 of 2020 and today I finished week 5 of the Nike 5k Training Plan I am following.

Here are the stats from my Garmin watch:

As you can see I managed to go on 5 runs, as per the training plan those were 2 tempo/interval runs, 2 recovery runs and 1 long run. The interval run on Tuesday was slightly longer than the actual long run, but thats to be expected on these “short” distances I guess.
I am pretty happy with how this week went down, weather was around 15-22°C for most of the week, just about right for me, but windy and rainy on 2 of the days. The only thing that felt off was the pacing, I went all over the place. Not sure if I somehow get influenced by the music I am listening to while the Audio Guides are silent or if I just suck at sticking to a pace in general, but that’s definitely something to work on, other than me being slow.

Next week the planend long run is for 6.5km or 4 Miles which is around the distance I ran before I stopped running over a year ago.
The tempo/interval runs in this training plan all have a 5 min warmup followed by optional stretching before the speed part which I’ve used to do some quick stretches which I remember from soccer practice almost 2 decades ago. I should look into what things to do for warmup, including for long and recovery runs.

This is all so far. Thank for reading this post. See you next Sunday (or maybe earlier).
